WATERMELON LEMONADE

home

description

Elevate classic lemonade using watermelon for a cool, fruity twist. Reviewers like how versatile this recipe is, allowing them to blend in strawberries and peaches.

  • 4 cups cubed watermelon
  • ½ cup white sugar
  • ½ cup water
  • 3 cups cold water
  • ½ cup fresh lemon juice
  • 6 cups ice cubes
